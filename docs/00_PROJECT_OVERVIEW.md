---

## Background

RelayOps is a one-person AI automation agency founded by Brian Chebitoch, a first-year university student based in Nairobi, Kenya. It was created on the principle that a service business reaches revenue faster than a SaaS product, and that the right way to build credibility is through real engineering work — not fabricated testimonials or inflated metrics.

The business model is simple: identify agencies and service businesses that lose deals to slow manual lead follow-up, build automation systems that fix that problem, get paid, and use each client's results to acquire the next.

## Non-Negotiable Constraints

These are carried forward from day one and must never be relaxed, regardless of pressure:

1. **Never fabricate** client names, logos, testimonials, metrics, or case studies presented as real.
2. **Never misrepresent AI involvement** in any deliverable — RelayOps' own site must not imply it was built differently than it was.
3. **Proof over claims** — if credibility does not yet exist, create it through real engineering work, not inflated marketing copy.
