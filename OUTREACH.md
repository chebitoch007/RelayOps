## How to Find 50 Businesses This Week

### Step 1 — Pick ONE niche (start here)
- Marketing agencies (easiest — they understand ROI)
- Real estate agents
- Dentists / medical clinics
- Coaches and consultants

### Step 2 — Find businesses
- Google: "marketing agency [city name]"
- LinkedIn: search job title "Founder" + "Marketing Agency"
- Clutch.co — directory of agencies with contact info
- Apollo.io — free tier gives 50 leads/month with emails

### Step 3 — Find their email
- Try: hello@, info@, [firstname]@
- Hunter.io — free plan finds email addresses
- LinkedIn DM if email not found

### Step 4 — Personalize each email
Change at minimum:
- Their first name
- Their company name
- One specific detail about their business (from their website)

### Step 5 — Send and track
Use Gmail + a spreadsheet:
| Name | Company | Email | Sent date | Reply? | Status |

## What to Do When Someone Replies

Reply the same day. Always.

If they say yes to a call:
1. Send a Calendly link (free at calendly.com)
2. Prepare: know their business before the call
3. On the call: listen more than you talk. Ask "What does your current process look like?"
4. Propose ONE specific automation. Quote a price.

Suggested first project price: $300–$800 (small scope, prove yourself, get testimonial)
